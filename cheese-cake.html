Cheese Cake

Ingredients:

  • Cream Cheese (softened): 2 packages (16 oz each)
  • Granulated Sugar: 1 cup
  • Sour Cream: 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs: 4 large eggs
  • Graham Cracker Crumbs: 1 1/2 cups
  • Unsalted Butter (melted): 1/3 cup
  • Strawberry Jam (optional): For topping
  • Fresh Strawberries (optional): For garnish

Click to prepare

Recipe:

  1. Preheat the Oven:

    Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan and set aside.

  2. Prepare Crust:

    In a mixing bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs and melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared springform pan to form the crust.

  3. Make Filling:

    In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and granulated sugar until smooth. Add the sour cream and vanilla extract, and beat until well combined.

    Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until fully incorporated into the cream cheese mixture.

  4. Pour and Bake:

    Pour the cream cheese filling over the prepared crust in the springform pan. Smooth the top with a spatula.

    Bake in the preheated oven for 55-60 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set and the edges are lightly golden brown.

  5. Cool and Chill:

    Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool in the pan on a wire rack for about 1 hour.

    Once cooled, cover the cheesecake with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to chill and set.

  6. Serve:

    When ready to serve, remove the cheesecake from the springform pan and transfer it to a serving plate. If desired, top with strawberry jam and fresh strawberries for garnish.

    Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your delicious homemade cheesecake!
