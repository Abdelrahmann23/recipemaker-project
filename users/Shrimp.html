Shrimps Cocktail Recipe

Ingredients:

    • Shrimp
    • Cocktail Sauce
    • Lemon Wedges
    • Garnish
    Click to serve

    Recipe:

    1. Cook the Shrimp:
      1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
      2. Add the shrimp and cook until they turn pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes.
      3. Drain the shrimp and transfer them to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process.
      4. Once cooled, drain the shrimp and refrigerate until ready to serve.
    2. Prepare the Cocktail Sauce:
      1. In a small bowl, mix together the ketchup, horseradish, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce (if using).
      2. Taste and adjust the seasoning to your liking, adding more horseradish or hot sauce for extra heat if desired.
      3. Cover and refrigerate the cocktail sauce until ready to serve.
    3. Assemble the Shrimp Cocktail:
      1. Arrange the chilled shrimp on a serving platter or individual cocktail glasses.
      2. Place a small bowl of cocktail sauce in the center of the platter or distribute it among the glasses.
      3. Garnish the shrimp cocktail with lemon wedges and chopped parsley.
    4. Serve:
      1. Serve the shrimp cocktail immediately, accompanied by lemon wedges and additional cocktail sauce on the side.
