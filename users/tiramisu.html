Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • Espresso or Strong Coffee: 240ml (about 1 cup)
  • Ladyfingers (Savoiardi Biscuits): 200g (about 24 pieces)
  • Mascarpone Cheese: 500g
  • Heavy Cream: 240ml (about 1 cup)
  • Granulated Sugar: 100g
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Cocoa Powder: For dusting
  • Dark Chocolate (for garnish): Optional

Recipe:

  1. Brew Espresso or Coffee:

    Prepare strong coffee or espresso and let it cool to room temperature.

  2. Prepare Mascarpone Mixture:

    In a mixing bowl, combine mascarpone cheese, heavy cream, granulated sugar, and vanilla extract.

    Use a hand mixer or stand mixer to beat the mixture until smooth and creamy.

  3. Assemble Tiramisu:

    Dip each ladyfinger into the cooled coffee/espresso for a few seconds, making sure they are soaked but not overly soggy.

    Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers at the bottom of a serving dish (such as a square baking dish or individual glasses).

  4. Add Mascarpone Layer:

    Spread a layer of the mascarpone mixture over the soaked ladyfingers, covering them evenly.

  5. Repeat Layers:

    Continue layering soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture until you reach the top of the dish, ending with a layer of the mascarpone mixture on top.

  6. Chill:

    Cover the assembled tiramisu with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.

  7. Dust with Cocoa Powder:

    Before serving, remove the tiramisu from the refrigerator and dust the top evenly with cocoa powder.

  8. Garnish with Chocolate Shavings (Optional):

    If desired, grate or shave dark chocolate over the top of the tiramisu for an extra touch of indulgence.

  9. Serve:

    Slice the tiramisu into portions and serve chilled. Enjoy the creamy, coffee-flavored dessert with its rich mascarpone layers!
