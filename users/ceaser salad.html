Ceaser Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce (1 head)
  • Croutons (homemade or store-bought)
  • Parmesan cheese (freshly grated)
  • Caesar dressing (homemade or store-bought)
  • Optional: Grilled chicken breast

Click to serve

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Dressing:

    In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, minced garlic, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, minced anchovy fillets (or anchovy paste), grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper until well combined.

    Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously until the dressing is smooth and creamy. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  2. Prepare the Salad:

    Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly. Tear or chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl.

    Add croutons to the bowl, tossing them with the lettuce.

    Grate fresh Parmesan cheese over the top of the salad.

  3. Assemble the Salad:

    Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the salad, starting with a small amount and adding more as desired.

    Toss the salad gently until all the ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.

    If using grilled chicken breast, arrange the sliced chicken on top of the salad.

  4. Serve:

    Divide the Caesar salad onto individual plates or serve it family-style in a large bowl.

    Optionally, garnish with additional grated Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.

    Serve immediately and enjoy!
