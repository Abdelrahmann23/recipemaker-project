Bruscheta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Baguette slices
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Fresh basil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Click to serve

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Tomato Topping:

    In a medium bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, minced garlic, chopped basil, extra virgin olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

    Set aside to marinate for at least 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Prepare the Bread:

    Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the sliced baguette on a baking sheet in a single layer. Brush each slice lightly with olive oil on both sides.

    Bake in the preheated oven for about 8-10 minutes, or until the bread slices are golden and crisp.

    Remove from the oven and let cool slightly

  3. Assemble the Bruschetta:

    Once the bread slices have cooled slightly, spoon a generous amount of the tomato topping onto each slice.

    Optionally, drizzle each bruschetta with a little balsamic glaze for extra flavor.

    If desired, sprinkle some shredded Parmesan cheese over the top of each bruschetta for added richness

  4. Serve:

    Arrange the bruschetta on a serving platter and serve immediately as an appetizer or snack.
