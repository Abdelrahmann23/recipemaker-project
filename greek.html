Greek Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

    • Romaine lettuce or mixed greens
    • Cucumber
    • Tomatoes
    • Red onion
    • Kalamata olives
    • Feta cheese
    • Greek dressing (olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper)
    Click to serve

    Recipe:

    1. In a large salad bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, bell pepper, and olives.
    2. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the vegetables.
    3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar or lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
    4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
    5. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    6. Serve the Greek salad immediately as a side dish or as a light and refreshing main course.
