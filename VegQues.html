Vegetable Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • Flour tortillas
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Olive oil (for cooking)



Click to prepare

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables:

    Slice the bell peppers and onions thinly. Slice the mushrooms.

  2. Sauté Vegetables:

    Heat a bit of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms. Sauté until the vegetables are softened and slightly caramelized, approximately 5-7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Assemble Quesadillas:

    Lay a flour tortilla flat on a clean surface. Sprinkle shredded cheese evenly over one half of the tortilla. Spoon the sautéed vegetables over the cheese. Fold the tortilla in half to cover the filling, creating a half-moon shape.

  4. Cook Quesadillas:

    Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place the assembled quesadilla in the skillet and cook until the bottom is golden brown and crispy, about 2-3 minutes. Carefully flip the quesadilla and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.

  5. Serve:

    Remove the quesadilla from the skillet and place it on a cutting board. Cut into wedges and serve hot with salsa and sour cream on the side.
