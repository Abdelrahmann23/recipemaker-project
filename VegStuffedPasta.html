Cheese & Vegetable Stuffed Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta shells
  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Cheese



Click to prepare

Recipe:

  1. Cook the Pasta Shells:

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta shells and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    While the pasta is cooking, prepare the filling. Finely chop the spinach, mushrooms, and onions. You can sauté the vegetables in a pan with a bit of olive oil until they are softened and any excess moisture has evaporated. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  3. Mix with Cheese:

    Once the vegetables are cooked, transfer them to a mixing bowl and let them cool slightly. Add the cheese of your choice to the bowl and mix well to combine. You can use shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, or any other cheese that melts well.

  4. Stuff the Pasta Shells:

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Take each cooked pasta shell and stuff it with the vegetable and cheese mixture. Fill each shell generously with the filling, pressing it in firmly.

  5. Arrange in Baking Dish:

    Lightly grease a baking dish with olive oil or cooking spray. Arrange the stuffed pasta shells in a single layer in the baking dish.

  6. Bake:

    Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  7. Serve:

    Remove the foil from the baking dish and let the stuffed pasta shells cool slightly before serving. You can garnish with fresh herbs like chopped parsley or basil if desired.


    Enjoy your homemade stuffed pasta! Feel free to customize the filling with your favorite vegetables and cheese to suit your taste preferences.
