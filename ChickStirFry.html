Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Rice or noodles
  • Snap peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sesame oil

Click to prepare

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Chicken:

    Thinly slice the chicken breast and set it aside. This will ensure quick and even cooking when stir-fried.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables:

    Cut the bell peppers into strips and separate the broccoli into small florets. Thinly slice the carrots lengthwise for a pleasant texture. Trim the ends off the snap peas for a clean presentation.

  3. Make the Sauce:

    In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce with finely minced garlic and ginger. This simple yet flavorful sauce will coat the chicken and vegetables beautifully.

  4. Cook the Chicken:

    Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the sliced chicken breast and sauté until it's nicely browned and fully cooked.

    After cooking, remove the chicken from the pan and keep it warm.

  5. Stir-Fry the Vegetables:

    In the same pan, add a touch more sesame oil if necessary. Sauté the prepared vegetables over high heat until they reach that perfect tender-crisp stage.

  6. Combine and Finish Cooking:

    Return the cooked chicken to the pan with the vegetables. Pour the previously made sauce over the mixture, ensuring an even distribution of flavors.

    Stir everything together and continue to cook for a few minutes, allowing the sauce to warm up and cling to each ingredient.

  7. Final Touches and Serving:

    Once the dish is heated through and the sauce has thickened slightly, it's ready to serve. For a complete meal, serve the chicken stir-fry atop a bed of steamed rice or your favorite type of noodles.
