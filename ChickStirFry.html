Ingredients you'll need:

  • Chicken breast
  • Bell peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Rice or noodles
  • Snap peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sesame oil


Preparation Of This Dish:

  1. Slice the chicken breast thinly and set aside.
  2. Cut the bell peppers into slices, broccoli into florets, and thinly slice the carrots.
  3. Trim the snap peas.
  4. In a bowl, mix soy sauce with minced garlic and ginger to create the sauce.
  5. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  6. Add the sliced chicken breast to the pan and cook until browned and cooked through.
  7. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  8. In the same pan, add a bit more sesame oil if needed and stir-fry the vegetables until they are tender-crisp.
  9. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
  10. Stir everything together and cook for a few more minutes until heated through and the sauce coats everything evenly.
  11. Serve the chicken stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.
