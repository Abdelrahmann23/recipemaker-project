Beef and Vegetable Curry

Ingredients:

  • Beef (flank steak or sirloin)
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Peas
  • Coconut milk
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Curry powder
  • Turmeric
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Rice (for serving)
Click to prepare

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    Cut the beef into bite-sized pieces. Peel and dice the potatoes and carrots. Slice the bell peppers. Mince the garlic and ginger.

  2. Sear the Beef:

    Heat a bit of olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the beef pieces and season with salt and black pepper. Sear the beef on all sides until browned. Remove the beef from the pot and set aside.

  3. Sauté Aromatics:

    In the same pot, add a bit more olive oil if needed. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and sauté for a minute until fragrant.

  4. Add Vegetables:

    Add the diced potatoes and carrots to the pot, and cook for a few minutes until they start to soften slightly.

  5. Add Spices:

    Stir in the curry powder and turmeric, and cook for another minute to toast the spices and release their flavors.

  6. Simmer:

    Return the seared beef to the pot. Pour in the coconut milk and add the sliced bell peppers and peas. Stir everything together until well combined.

  7. Cook the Curry:

    Bring the curry to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the curry simmer gently for about 20-25 minutes, or until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.

  8. Adjust Seasoning:

    Taste the curry and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.

  9. Serve:

    Serve the beef and vegetable curry hot over cooked rice. Garnish with chopped cilantro or parsley if desired, and serve with naan bread or other flatbreads on the side.


    Enjoy your homemade beef and vegetable curry! Adjust the spice level and vegetable selection according to your taste preferences.
